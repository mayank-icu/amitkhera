FAQ

1What is pre-wedding photography?

Pre-wedding photography is a photoshoot that takes place before the wedding day. It is a great way for the couple to capture some special moments together before the wedding and get comfortable in front of the camera.

2What is included in your pre-wedding photography packages?

Our pre-wedding photography packages include a photoshoot at a location of your choice, professional editing and retouching of the images, and a digital gallery of high-resolution images that you can download and share.

3Can we choose the location for our pre-wedding photoshoot?

Yes, you can choose any location for your pre-wedding photoshoot. We can also suggest some beautiful and picturesque locations if you need help deciding.

4 How long does a pre-wedding photoshoot usually take?

A pre-wedding photoshoot can take anywhere from 2-4 hours depending on the location and the number of outfit changes you plan to make.

5 When should we book our pre-wedding photoshoot?

We recommend booking your pre-wedding photoshoot at least 3-4 months before your wedding day to ensure availability and give enough time for editing and printing of the images.
