Why Budgeting for Wedding Photography Is Important

Weddings are emotional and visual events, and capturing them professionally is a must. However, wedding expenses add up quickly, and without careful planning, photography costs can spiral beyond expectations. By setting a photography budget early and knowing average prices, you'll be better equipped to shortlist the right professionals for your big day.

What Is the Average Wedding Shoot Price in 2025?

In 2025, the average wedding shoot price in India ranges from ₹60,000 to ₹3,00,000 depending on factors such as:

  • Location
  • Photographer's experience
  • Type of shoot (traditional, candid, cinematic)
  • Number of events covered
  • Deliverables (albums, teaser videos, drone footage)

Here's a breakdown:

Package Type Average Cost (INR)
Basic Traditional Shoot ₹50,000 – ₹80,000
Candid & Cinematic Combo ₹90,000 – ₹1,80,000
Full Wedding Coverage ₹1,50,000 –₹3,00,000+
Pre-Wedding Shoot ₹25,000 – ₹75,000

The pricing also reflects regional differences. While metropolitan cities may command higher rates, cities like Dehradun offer great talent at more affordable prices.

Factors That Influence Wedding Photography Costs

Several factors impact how much you'll pay for wedding photography:

1. Experience and Reputation

Highly experienced photographers with a solid portfolio may charge more, but their quality and reliability often justify the premium.

2. Number of Events

Is it a one-day wedding or a 3-day celebration with haldi, mehndi, sangeet, and reception? More events require more time, effort, and team support.

3. Type of Photography

Candid and cinematic photography often costs more than traditional styles due to specialized equipment and post-production work.

4. Travel and Accommodation

For destination weddings, additional costs like travel, stay, and food are usually borne by the client.

5. Deliverables

Albums, reels, teasers, raw footage, and edited videos all add to the overall cost.

How to Choose the Right Photographer Within Budget

Staying within your budget doesn't mean compromising on quality. Here's how you can get the best value:

  • Set Priorities: Decide if you want a pre-wedding shoot, drone shots, or just the main wedding day covered.
  • Compare Packages: Don't just look at price—check what's included.
  • Request Custom Quotes: Many photographers are open to tailoring packages.
  • Check Reviews and Portfolios: Look for consistency, clarity, and style that matches your taste.
  • Book Early: Many top-tier photographers get booked 6–12 months in advance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How much should I budget for wedding photography in India for 2025?
A: On average, allocate 10–15% of your total wedding budget to photography. For mid-range coverage, expect to spend ₹1,00,000–₹1,80,000.
Q2: Are pre-wedding shoots included in standard packages?
A: Not always. Pre-wedding shoots are usually charged separately. Some photographers, like Amit Khera Photography, offer bundled deals depending on your requirements.
Q3: What is the ideal timeline for booking a wedding photographer?
A: Book at least 6 months in advance to secure your preferred photographer, especially during peak wedding seasons (October to February).
Q4: Can I customize a package based on my wedding schedule?
A: Yes, most professional photographers provide flexible options that can be tailored to your event schedule and budget.
