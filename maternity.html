FAQ

1 When should I schedule my maternity shoot?

We recommend scheduling your maternity shoot around 28-32 weeks of pregnancy when your bump is prominent but before you become too uncomfortable.

2How long will the maternity shoot take?

The maternity shoot typically takes 1-2 hours, depending on the location and number of outfit changes.

3Can my partner and other children be included in the shoot?

Yes, we encourage including family members in the shoot to make it a memorable experience.

4What should I wear for my maternity shoot?

We recommend wearing form-fitting clothes that accentuate your bump, such as dresses, maxi skirts, or fitted tops. You can also bring along any props or accessories that are meaningful to you.

5 DHow do I prepare for the shoot?

We recommend getting plenty of rest, staying hydrated, and avoiding tight clothing or accessories that could leave marks on your skin.
