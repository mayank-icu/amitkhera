FAQ

1 Can I bring my own clothes to the fashion shoot?

Yes, you can bring your own clothes to the fashion shoot. It's recommended to bring multiple outfits to give more variety to your photos.

2 How long will the fashion shoot last?

The length of the fashion shoot will depend on the package you choose. Typically, it can range from 1-3 hours.

3Do you provide hair and makeup services for the fashion shoot?

We do not provide hair and makeup services, but we can recommend some talented professionals for you to work with.

4 How many edited photos can I expect from the fashion shoot?

The number of edited photos will depend on the package you choose. Typically, you can expect to receive 10-20 edited photos.

5 Can I bring props to the fashion shoot?

Yes, you can bring props to the fashion shoot, but please let us know in advance so we can plan accordingly.
