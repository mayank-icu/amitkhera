FAQ

1How long before our wedding should we schedule our engagement session?

It's recommended to schedule your engagement session at least 6-8 weeks before your wedding date.

2How many outfit changes can we have during our engagement session?

You can have up to 2 outfit changes during your engagement session.

3What if the weather is bad on the day of our engagement session?

If the weather is bad, we can reschedule your engagement session for another day at no additional cost.

4 Can we bring our pets to our engagement session?

Yes, you can bring your pets to your engagement session, but please let us know in advance so we can plan accordingly.

5 When can we expect to receive our engagement photos?

You can expect to receive your edited engagement photos within 2-3 weeks after your session.
